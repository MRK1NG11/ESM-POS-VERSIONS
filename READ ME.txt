🛠️ QUICK USER GUIDE
--------------------------------------------------
1. RINGING UP SALES: 
   Tap the category buttons (MEAT, VEG, DRINK) to filter products. Tap a product to add it to the cart. 

2. VOIDING ITEMS: 
   Select the item in the cart list and press "VOID". A manager PIN is required.

3. HOLDING ORDERS: 
   If a customer forgets their wallet, press "HOLD" to save their cart and clear the screen for the next customer. Press "RECALL" to bring it back.

4. EXACT CASH / CHANGE: 
   Use the +R10, +R50, etc., buttons to input the cash the customer hands you. The system will calculate the exact change due in green.

5. END OF DAY (Z-REPORT): 
   At closing, click "ADMIN PANEL", enter the manager PIN, and click "PRINT Z-REPORT". This will print the daily totals and package the data for the back-office.
